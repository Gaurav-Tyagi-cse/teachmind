Web Application XII

Welcome!

Web Applications for CBSE Class 12th students is a 100-Mark subject encompassing JavaScript Multimedia and Employability Skills . The examination structure comprises a 60-Mark theory paper conducted at designated examination centers and a 40-Mark practical paper administered at schools under the supervision of an external examiner.

Prerequisites

Before delving into Web Application-12th, it's essential to have a basic understanding of operating systems, along with familiarity with JavaScript ,HTM concepts covered in Class XI.

Learning Objectives

Students will acquire fundamental expertise in JavaScript, HTML to develop a website, laying a solid foundation for advanced studies in the IT sector or pursuing degrees such as BCA, B.Sc(IT), or MCA, thereby significantly enhancing career prospects in the field..
