Facts About Tech MindGuru

Established in 2009 by Mr. Prabhat Lohani, Tech MindGuru is an ISO-certified institution renowned for its expertise in programming training. We offer comprehensive courses covering a range of programming languages and technologies including C, C++, Python, Java, Data Structures, R, Full Stack Web Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. Our programs are specifically tailored for CBSE class XI-XII students, graduates, undergraduates, and working professionals. With a specialized focus on programming languages, Tech MindGuru has successfully trained over numerous individuals, many of whom have achieved excellent results in CBSE board examinations. Additionally, our graduates have secured coveted positions in leading IT companies. Located in Delhi, India, Tech MindGuru stands as a prominent destination for programming education and career advancement.
