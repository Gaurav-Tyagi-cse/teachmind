Informatics Technology XII

Welcome!

Information Technology for CBSE Class 12th students is a 100-Mark subject encompassing Java Programming, Database Management(MySQL), Operating Web Based Applications, Work Integrated Learning IT-DMA, and Employability Skills . The examination structure comprises a 60-Mark theory paper conducted at designated examination centers and a 40-Mark practical paper administered at schools under the supervision of an external examiner.

Prerequisites

Before delving into Information Technology-12th, it's essential to have a basic understanding of operating systems, along with familiarity with Java concepts covered in Class XI.

Learning Objectives

Students will acquire fundamental expertise in java programming languages, database management, and Operating Web Based Applications, laying a solid foundation for advanced studies in the IT sector or pursuing degrees such as BCA, B.Sc(IT), or MCA, thereby significantly enhancing career prospects in the field.
