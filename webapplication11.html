Web Application XI

Welcome!

Web Applications for CBSE Class 11th students is a 100-Mark subject encompassing JavaScript ,HTML,CSS , Multimedia and Employability Skills . The examination structure comprises a 60-Mark theory paper conducted at designated examination centers and a 40-Mark practical paper administered at schools under the supervision of an external examiner.

Prerequisites

Before delving into Web Application-11th, it's essential to have a basic understanding of operating systems.

Learning Objectives

Students will acquire fundamental expertise in JavaScript, HTML to develop a website, laying a solid foundation for advanced studies in the IT sector or pursuing degrees such as BCA, B.Sc(IT), or MCA, thereby significantly enhancing career prospects in the field.
